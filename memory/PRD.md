# WA.Bilvård - Premium Car Detailing Website

## Project Overview
Modern, professional, and conversion-focused website for WA.Bilvård, a premium car detailing service in Löddeköpinge, Sweden.

## Business Information
- **Business Name**: WA.Bilvård
- **Industry**: Car detailing & car care services
- **Rating**: 4.9⭐ (104 Google reviews)
- **Address**: Backavägen 3, 246 31 Löddeköpinge, Sweden
- **Phone**: 076-037 77 72
- **Email**: kontakt@wabilvard.se

## User Personas
1. **Car Owners**: Individuals seeking professional car detailing services
2. **Luxury Car Owners**: Premium vehicle owners looking for high-quality care
3. **Local Customers**: Residents in Löddeköpinge and surrounding areas

## Core Requirements
- Professional, trustworthy, premium brand presentation
- Clean Scandinavian design aesthetic
- Mobile-first and fully responsive
- Clear booking CTAs (Phone, Email, SMS)
- Social proof (reviews and ratings)
- Location information with map
- Service showcase
